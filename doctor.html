Here's what you need to know about our medical staff
Fully registered AHPRA

Rest assured, our doctors are licensed and endorsed by governmental and independent bodies. They bring extensive experience from emergency rooms, departments, surgeries, or pediatric units, ensuring top-notch treatment and outcomes.

Experienced former hospital, GP or GP registrar doctors

All of our doctors have proven track records gained from operating on the front line of healthcare in Australia. This means all of our medical staff are well-versed in working within hospitals, doctors surgeries or other medical institutions.

Warm, approachable and knowledgeable, with excellent interpersonal skills

A key duty for any doctor is translating medical jargon into straightforward and easily understandable terms for patients. What’s more, a doctor must be able to work compassionately with patients during trying times. All of our doctors have the warmth and kindness necessary to fulfill this role.

Committed to ongoing learning and development, staying on top of any changes in their field

Our doctor’s do not stay still. Nor do they rest on their laurels. Instead, the work to better themselves and improve their understanding of the profession. The medical world is changing all the time; a commitment to learning and development is vital.

Patient-oriented, and committed to going above and beyond to achieve the results the patient needs

The patient always comes first at 13CURE. So it is no surprise that our doctors are all 100% committed to meeting and surpassing our patients’ expectations.
